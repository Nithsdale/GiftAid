  • About
  • My name is Lindsay. I am a full time mother, wife, employee, and student. I normally am a very busy person so things tend to slip my mind. As I'm sure that happens to all of us, no matter how good of a memory you have, we all need help sometimes. Some occasions tend to pop up out of nowhere, and if you have a big family or a large circle of friends, birthdays, anniversaries, or other such important occasions can be forgotten. Which is why I created this app. Add as many people as you want, all the gift giving occasions you want to remember, set your reminders, and let the app do the work for you. Jot down quick ideas and use the shopping feature for online shopping. Share your list with others and never forget a gift giving occasion again!
